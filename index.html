Venezuela

Maracaibo It's the capital of Zulia State, the second biggest city in Venezuela. One of the most enchanting aspects of Maracaibo is the Catatumbo lightning. this natural phenomenon is like a never-ending thunderstorm where lightning strikes up to 40 times a minute. Maracaibo is known as the Beloved Land of the Sun, reflecting the warmth of the culture and the warmth of its sunny weather that paint beautiful sunsets over South America's largest lake, Maracaibo's Lake.
